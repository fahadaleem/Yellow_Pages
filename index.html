General Info

Here at Seven's Mediterranean Turkish Grill we use the freshest ingredients. None of the food is processed or frozen. Everything from our kebabs to our pita's are made fresh and in house. Our pita's have the perfect thickness and shape and are slathered with meats, cheeses, or vegetables, and sometimes all three! Our zucchini pancakes, served with a deliciously thick yogurt garlic sauce are an instant classic. Try one of our kebabs where you can mix and match each to savor the flavor. We offer delicious homemade specialties at prices under $20.We also make the best deserts that are prepared daily. Come on in or give us a call to cater your next party. There is also a daily 2 course lunch special with a complimentary glass of wine for $12.99.
